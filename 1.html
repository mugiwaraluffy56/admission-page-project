Guidelines for Filling the Admission Form :

  • Fields marked with an asterisk * are mandatory.
  • All information must be accurate and match your official documents.
  • Use proper spelling and capitalization for names and addresses.
  • Ensure your email ID and phone number are working and accessible.
  • For dropdown fields, select the most relevant option. If unsure, select “Other”.
  • Review each section before moving to the next part.
  • Incomplete forms may be rejected or not processed.
  • If you face any technical issues while filling the form, contact the admission office immediately.


Personal Details :

Important Guidelines for Personal Details :

  • Fill in your full name as per the official records (e.g., school certificates, identity proof). Ensure the first letter of each name is capitalized.
  • Select your gender from the options provided (Male, Female, or Other). Please choose the option that reflects your identity.
  • Enter your date of birth in the correct format (DD/MM/YYYY) as per your official documents.
  • Provide a valid email address that you frequently check. This email will be used for any further communication related to the admission process.
  • Enter a working phone number, including the country code, which we can use to reach you if needed.












Address :

Important Guidelines for address section :

  • Fill in your current address where you currently reside. This is important for communication purposes, so ensure it is accurate.
  • If your permanent address is different from your current address, please fill it in here. If both addresses are the same, you may leave this section blank.
  • Enter the name of the city/town where you reside. Be sure to spell the name correctly.
  • Select the state where your city or town is located. Please capitalize the first letter of the state name.
  • Enter the correct postal code (PIN) for your address. Verify the accuracy to ensure proper mail delivery.
